Why Switchless oﬃces

Buildings do not consume energy, people do.

Lighting and Airconditioning together consume about 80% of energy in a typical oﬃce. There is a large scope to make the usage of this energy more eﬃcient. Infrequently used areas, especially in the public and commercial domain have their Lighting,Fans, ACs, Exhausts etc switched on most of the time and monitoring the same is both time consuming and labour intensive. Ambient light is also not generally utilized to save energy. Further with the advent of global warming and environmental awareness it has become imperative that this enormous issue has to be resolved.

Introducing ILMS

While Switchless Oﬃces™ is the solutions part of our business, ILMS™ is our product range with which the solution is implemented.

ILMS™ or Intelligent Lighting Management System is an innovative and unique Sensor based Light/Energy Management System designed to save energy in infrequently used spaces like Oﬃces, Showrooms, Warehouses, Basement Car parks, Corridors, Conference and Meeting Rooms, Oﬃce Cubicles, Restrooms, Storage areas etc. With ILMS™ you can control lights, fans, exhaust systems, ACs (including Splits, VRV, VAV), etc at multiple areas and ensure that they always operate eﬃciently every time.

How it works

ILMS™ uses Motion sensors and Ambient Light Sensors (ALS) to detect people and available natural light, and using our customised and preprogrammed algorithms controls the given space in the most eﬃcient manner.

How We Do It

With a new facility we come in at the planning stage and design the controls for the lighting layout in consultation with the Client, Architect and the Electrical Consultants. We then work with the electrical contractors to get the required wiring. After that we install our controllers and sensors with custom programming for the work space. When it comes to retrofit we undertake or contract the required rewiring and then install our controllers and sensors
